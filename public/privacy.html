Lumen is a browser extension and web tool that analyzes argument structure in articles using AI.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or sell personal data. Article text submitted for analysis is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing and is not stored on our servers.

Rate Limiting

We use IP-based rate limiting (via Cloudflare Workers) to prevent abuse. IP addresses are stored temporarily in KV storage for rate limiting only and are automatically deleted after 24 hours.

Local Storage

Analysis history is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time.

Third-Party Services

Article text is sent to Google's Gemini API for AI analysis. Google's privacy policy applies to their processing of this data. See: https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms

No Tracking

We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking scripts.
